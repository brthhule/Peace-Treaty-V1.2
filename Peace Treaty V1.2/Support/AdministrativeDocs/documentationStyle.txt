1) Namespace Sections
	1.1) Use long "//" blocks to indicate sections-- see 1.11
		1.11) //////////SECTION_NAME///////////
		1.1.2) The sections in 1.11 shall extend to 80 characters with SECTION_NAME 
		formatted approximately in the middle of the comment block
		1.1.3) SECTION_NAME shall start at the 40 column mark
	1.2) For subsections, use "//" followed by 4 "-" symbols, the subsection
	name, and then additional "-" symbols followed to extend to the 40 
	column line
		1.3.1) //----SUBSECTION_NAME----
	1.3) All words in section/subsection titles shall be capitalized
